iTravel Features

Realtime & Adaptive
iTravel tracks if you follow the travel plan and updates in realtime to your actual plan, offering you new choices to choose from if your route deviates significantly from your original plan, allowing you the flexibility and independence to go where you want, when you want, and still have a travel assistant to help you when you need it.

Personalized
iTravel lets you choose which attractions/venues to visit and when to visit them. You can also specify additional parameters for the type and price range of hotels, restaurants, and many more. iTravel is about creating a plan that fits you perfectly.

Local Gems
iTravel has a built-in feature that highlights local gems, promoting local businesses and helping travelers discover smaller and less-visible locations or venues, which helps dissipate crowds and improve the travel experience for each individual traveller. This also allows you the flexibility to visit new places that may not typically be highlighted on large-group tourism routes.

Web & Mobile Optimized
iTravel offers both a rich web-based application optimized for original travel planning and research alongside a mobile application tailored for on-the-go tracking and realtime updates.
